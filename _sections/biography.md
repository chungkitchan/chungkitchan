Chloe Chan is a recent graduate of **Berklee College of Music**, holding a Bachelor of Arts in **Music Composition for Film, TV and Games**. Her musical journey began at age four when she started piano, going on to pass numerous exams with **ABRSM** and **YAMAHA**.

During secondary school she joined the **Symphonic Band CCA** as a clarinetist — a passion she has carried from 2015 to the present. Since then she has earned multiple diplomas in both music performance and composition.

Chloe is also a proud member of **NOVOwinds**, performing as clarinetist and pianist, and occasionally creating arrangements and co-producing their promotional trailers. Outside the studio she works as a dedicated **piano teacher**.
